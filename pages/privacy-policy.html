Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

RankerToolAI ("we," "us," "our") operates rankertoolai.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information automatically when you visit the site:

  • Analytics data: Pages visited, time on page, referral source, browser type, and device type via Google Analytics. This data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Affiliate click data: When you click an affiliate link, we log the tool name, timestamp, and referral page for commission tracking. We do not collect personally identifiable information from these clicks.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and to maintain affiliate attribution. See Section 4 for details.

We do not collect your name, email address, or payment information (we have no account system or checkout).

2. How We Use Information

  • To understand how visitors use our site and improve content
  • To track affiliate referrals for commission purposes
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising targeting.

3. Affiliate Links

Our site contains affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we earn a commission. Affiliate partners may set their own cookies to track referrals — their use of your data is governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics: _ga, _gid, _gat — anonymized analytics tracking. Expires: 2 years / 24 hours / 1 minute respectively.
  • Affiliate tracking: Set by third-party affiliate networks when you click our links.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using the site but may affect affiliate attribution.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare: CDN and security. Cloudflare Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete data we hold about you. Since we collect minimal data and no personally identifiable information directly, most requests can be addressed by clearing cookies in your browser.

For any privacy requests, contact us at: privacy@rankertoolai.com

7. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months. Affiliate click logs are retained for 90 days for commission reconciliation.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of changes.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@rankertoolai.com
