Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026 · Effective: June 19, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how RankerToolAI ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit rankertoolai.com. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide website owners with information about how their sites are being used.

We also use similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage that function similarly to cookies. This policy covers all these technologies collectively when we refer to "cookies."

4. How We Obtain Your Consent

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner is displayed at the bottom of the screen. You can choose to:

  • Accept All Cookies — enables all cookie categories including analytics and marketing cookies
  • Necessary Only — accepts only strictly necessary cookies required for the site to function

Your preference is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key rta_consent and respected on all subsequent visits. Analytics cookies (GA4, Clarity) are only loaded after you accept them.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these third parties' use of cookies. The following third parties may set cookies when you use our site:

  • Google Analytics — Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity — Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • Formsubmit.co — used for newsletter and contact form submissions. Formsubmit Privacy Policy

When you click affiliate links on our site (marked with disclosure), you are redirected to third-party websites which may set their own cookies. We are not responsible for those cookies.

6. How to Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies (except strictly necessary ones). You can exercise your cookie preferences in several ways:

  • Our cookie banner: Use the consent banner shown on your first visit to make your selection. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage and revisiting our site.
  • Browser settings: Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set, visit www.aboutcookies.org.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Microsoft Clarity opt-out: You can opt out of Clarity at clarity.microsoft.com/optout.

Note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not function as intended.

7. Cookies and Performance

We use analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity) to understand how our content is performing. This includes:

  • Which pages are most visited and how long users spend on them
  • How users navigate between pages (session recording via Clarity)
  • Which traffic sources bring users to our site
  • Geographic distribution of our audience

This data is used exclusively to improve the quality and relevance of our content. We do not sell this data to third parties.

8. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want your online activity tracked. Our website does not currently respond differently to DNT signals, as there is no consistent industry standard for doing so. However, you can use our cookie consent mechanism to limit tracking.
