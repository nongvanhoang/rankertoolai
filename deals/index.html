How to Get the Best AI Tool Deals

  • Always try the free plan first — most top AI tools offer a meaningful free tier before paying
  • Choose annual billing — saves 17–25% vs monthly on most platforms
  • Watch for Black Friday/Cyber Monday — AI tools commonly offer 30–50% off in November
  • Check AppSumo — occasionally offers lifetime deals on newer AI tools (NeuronWriter, SEO tools)
  • Use trials strategically — start your free trial just before a big project to maximize the test period
  • Team plans have better per-seat pricing — if you have 2+ users, team plans are usually cheaper than multiple solo subscriptions
