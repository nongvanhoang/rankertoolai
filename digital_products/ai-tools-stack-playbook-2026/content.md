## Why a "stack" instead of one tool?

No single AI tool does everything well. The fastest way to actually ship work — a video, a newsletter, an SEO campaign, a data report — is combining 2-4 tools that each do one thing best. This playbook gives you 4 pre-built stacks based on the most common goals we see, plus honest notes on when to swap a tool out.

Every tool listed here is one we've personally reviewed at rankertoolai.com — full reviews linked at the end of each section.

## Stack 1: Content Creator / Video

**Goal:** Turn written content into video, without a camera, mic, or editing skill.

| Tool | Role in the stack | Starting price |
|---|---|---|
| **Pictory** | Turns blog posts, scripts, or URLs into finished videos automatically | Free trial, paid plans from ~$19/mo |
| **Synthesia** | AI avatar videos — 140+ languages, no camera needed | From ~$29/mo |
| **ElevenLabs** | Voice cloning + realistic text-to-speech for narration | From ~$22/mo |
| **Descript** | AI transcription, Overdub voice cloning, and edit video like a text doc | From ~$24/mo |

**How they fit together:** Draft the script → Pictory converts it to a rough video cut, or Synthesia turns it into a talking-avatar video → ElevenLabs generates the voiceover if you're not using an avatar's built-in voice → Descript does the final trim/cleanup and any corrections (edit the transcript, the video updates automatically).

**When to use less than all 4:** If you only need narration without video, ElevenLabs alone covers it. If you're already inside a video editor you like, Descript's Overdub + editing can replace the other three for simple talking-head content.
