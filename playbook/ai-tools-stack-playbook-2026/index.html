Stack 1: Content Creator / Video

Goal

Turn written content into video, without a camera, mic, or editing skill.

PictoryTurns blog posts, scripts, or URLs into finished videos automaticallyFree trial, from ~$19/mo
SynthesiaAI avatar videos — 140+ languages, no camera neededFrom ~$29/mo
ElevenLabsVoice cloning + realistic text-to-speech for narrationFrom ~$22/mo
DescriptAI transcription, Overdub voice cloning, edit video like a text docFrom ~$24/mo

How they fit together: Draft the script → Pictory converts it to a rough video cut, or Synthesia turns it into a talking-avatar video → ElevenLabs generates the voiceover if you're not using an avatar's built-in voice → Descript does the final trim/cleanup and any corrections (edit the transcript, the video updates automatically).

When to use less than all 4: If you only need narration without video, ElevenLabs alone covers it. If you're already inside a video editor you like, Descript's Overdub + editing can replace the other three for simple talking-head content.

How they fit together: SE Ranking or Mangools handles traditional keyword research and rank tracking — pick one, not both, they overlap (SE Ranking is deeper/more expensive, Mangools is the budget pick).
