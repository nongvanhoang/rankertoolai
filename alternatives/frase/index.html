Why People Switch From Frase

Frase is a legitimate tool — the content brief generation is fast and the pricing is accessible. But users frequently run into the same frustrations:

  • NLP scoring is less precise. Frase's Topic Score is based on topic coverage, not NLP frequency analysis. Many users find Surfer SEO's Content Score more directly tied to ranking improvements.
  • No keyword research tool. Frase has basic volume data but no standalone keyword explorer. You need a separate tool for keyword research, adding cost and workflow friction.
  • No site audit. Frase only handles content optimization — no technical SEO. You need a separate audit tool.
  • AI writing add-on cost. Frase's AI writing is a $35/month add-on. At $80/month total (Basic + AI), you are close to Surfer SEO's $89/month which includes better scoring and keyword research.
  • Limited rank tracking. Frase does not track keyword rankings over time. If you want to measure the results of your content optimization, you need another tool.

1. Surfer SEO — Best Overall Frase Alternative

Alternative #1
Surfer SEO
The most accurate content optimization tool for ranking
Best for: Content marketers serious about ranking

Surfer SEO is the most direct upgrade from Frase. Both tools optimize content against SERP competitors — but Surfer's Content Editor uses more refined NLP analysis, its scoring is more consistently tied to ranking improvements, and it includes keyword research and a site audit that Frase lacks.

The Content Editor is Surfer's core product: you type your keyword, Surfer analyzes the top 10+ SERP results, and generates a list of terms your article needs to include (with frequency targets), a recommended word count, heading structure suggestions, and a real-time Content Score from 0–100. The goal is 70+ to be competitive.

What Surfer does better than Frase: the NLP term extraction is more specific, the scoring correlation with actual rankings is stronger, and you get keyword research + site audit built in. Surfer AI can also generate complete optimized drafts — not just paragraph assists.

The tradeoff: $89/month vs Frase's $45/month. But when you factor in Frase's $35/month AI add-on, the price gap narrows considerably.

2. Semrush — Best Full SEO Suite Alternative

Alternative #2
Semrush
The all-in-one SEO platform with content tools built in
Best for: SEO professionals who want everything in one tool

If you are switching from Frase because you need more than content optimization, Semrush is the complete package — keyword research, competitor analysis, rank tracking, backlink data, site audit, AND content tools in one platform.

Semrush's content tools include the SEO Writing Assistant (Chrome extension + Google Docs integration with real-time content scoring), the SEO Content Template (generates recommendations before you write based on top-ranking competitors), and the Content Audit tool (analyzes existing content for quality and optimization opportunities).

The SEO Writing Assistant gives you a content score, recommended keywords, readability score, tone consistency, and originality check as you write in Google Docs. It is less detailed than Surfer SEO's Content Editor but gives you more dimensions of feedback and integrates with the rest of Semrush's data.

The main advantage over Frase: Semrush replaces 4–5 tools (Frase + a keyword tool + a rank tracker + a backlink checker + a site auditor) in one subscription. The main disadvantage: it is $139.95/month minimum, making it expensive if you only need content optimization.

3. Clearscope — Best for Enterprise Content Teams

Alternative #3
Clearscope
The most trusted content grader for large content operations
Best for: Enterprise teams and agencies with budget flexibility

Clearscope is the premium end of content optimization tools — used by enterprise teams at HubSpot, IBM, and major agencies. Its content grading (A+ to F scale) is highly accurate, its term suggestions are precise, and its Google Docs integration is seamless.

Like Surfer SEO, Clearscope analyzes SERP competitors and generates a list of terms to include with weighting by importance. The interface is clean and the reports are easy to share with writers. Unlike Frase, there is no AI writing — Clearscope is a pure optimization tool.

The downside: $170/month minimum with limited document credits, making it the most expensive pure content optimization tool. For large teams publishing frequently, the per-seat pricing model and document credit system can add up fast. For solo operators or small teams, it is hard to justify vs Surfer SEO at $89/month.

Jasper's Brand Voice feature trains on your existing content and ensures every piece sounds consistent — a major advantage for marketing teams. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Frase?

There is no true free alternative that matches Frase's content optimization features. Semrush and SE Ranking both offer free tiers with limited functionality. Surfer SEO offers a 7-day free trial. For completely free content optimization, Google Search Console combined with manual SERP analysis is the closest — time-consuming but costs nothing.

Is Surfer SEO better than Frase for ranking?

Yes, in our testing and based on community feedback, Surfer SEO's Content Score is more directly correlated with ranking improvements. The NLP term analysis is more precise and actionable. If ranking is your primary goal, Surfer SEO consistently outperforms Frase for content optimization. See our full Surfer SEO vs Frase comparison for details.

Does Semrush replace Frase?

Partially. Semrush's SEO Writing Assistant and SEO Content Template cover the core content optimization use case — generating keyword recommendations before writing and scoring content quality as you write. But Semrush's content tools are not as deep as Surfer SEO's Content Editor for NLP-level optimization. Semrush is better as a full SEO suite replacement than as a pure Frase replacement.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Frase with the same features?

SE Ranking at $52/month gives you content optimization scoring plus keyword research, rank tracking, and site audit — more features than Frase at $7/month more. NeuronWriter at $23/month is cheaper and provides NLP content scoring, though without the brief generation Frase excels at.

What is Frase best at that alternatives cannot match?

Frase's automated content brief generation — pulling competitor headings, questions, and topic coverage into a structured brief in under 2 minutes — is genuinely the best in its class. If brief generation for a team of writers is your primary use case, Frase is still the best tool for that specific workflow, even if alternatives beat it on content scoring depth.
