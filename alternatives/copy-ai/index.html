Frequently Asked Questions

Is Copy.ai free?

Yes — Copy.ai has a free plan with 2,000 words per month and access to basic templates and one user seat. This is enough for light use and testing. The free plan doesn't include Copy.ai's Workflows (automation) feature or multi-seat collaboration. For regular content production, paid plans starting at $36/month provide unlimited words.

Is Writesonic better than Copy.ai?

For long-form blog content, Writesonic's Article Writer 5.0 outperforms Copy.ai in most tests — producing more structured, SEO-ready articles. For short-form copy and marketing automation workflows, Copy.ai's Workflows feature is more powerful. Both are strong tools; the best choice depends on whether your primary need is long-form content (Writesonic) or systematic short-form copy at scale (Copy.ai).

What is Copy.ai Workflows?

Copy.ai Workflows is an AI automation feature that chains multiple content tasks together — for example: research a topic → generate an outline → write a blog post → create social media posts from the blog → write 5 email subject line variations for promotion. These workflows run automatically and can dramatically scale content production. It's one of Copy.ai's strongest differentiators from basic AI writing tools.

What is the best free alternative to Copy.ai?

Claude Free and ChatGPT Free both provide strong AI writing at zero cost — and both let you produce far more than 2,000 words per month. They lack Copy.ai's marketing templates but produce higher quality output with good prompting. For template-driven marketing copy at no cost, Simplified's free plan is worth trying.
