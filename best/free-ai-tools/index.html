Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free AI tool in 2026?

Claude (free tier) is the best free AI assistant — Claude Sonnet with daily limits at no cost. For research, Perplexity is free and excellent. For coding, GitHub Copilot's free tier (2,000 completions/month) is now available. For images, Bing Image Creator offers DALL-E 3 quality completely free.

Are free AI tools safe to use?

Generally yes, but review privacy policies. Free tools often use your inputs to improve their models. Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini let you disable training data use in settings. For sensitive business information, use paid plans with stronger privacy controls, or the API with data controls.

Which free AI tool is best for coding?

GitHub Copilot's free tier (launched late 2024) is the best free coding AI — 2,000 completions and 50 chat messages per month in VS Code and JetBrains. Codeium is the best unlimited-free alternative (no monthly cap for individual users).
