Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Summary: RankerToolAI uses affiliate links. If you click a link and buy, we may earn a commission — at no extra cost to you. This never influences our ratings or recommendations.

How We Make Money

RankerToolAI is an independent review and comparison website for AI tools and SaaS products. We earn revenue through affiliate partnerships with companies whose products we review.

When you click a link on our site marked as an "affiliate link" and subsequently make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may receive a commission from that company. These commissions help fund our editorial team and allow us to continue providing free, independent reviews.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are URLs that contain a unique tracking code identifying RankerToolAI as the referral source. When you follow one of these links, the company can see that you came from our site.

On RankerToolAI, affiliate links are:

  • Clearly labeled as "affiliate link" near the relevant button or link
  • Routed through our /go/[tool-name]/ redirect paths
  • Tagged with rel="nofollow sponsored" attributes in compliance with Google guidelines

Our Editorial Independence

Our editorial team operates independently from our commercial partnerships. This means:

  • We rate tools honestly. A tool's affiliate commission does not affect its rating on our site. We give low scores to tools that deserve low scores, even if they pay high commissions.
  • We recommend what we believe in. We only feature affiliate programs for tools we would genuinely recommend to our readers.
  • We disclose conflicts. If we have a direct commercial relationship with a company beyond standard affiliate marketing (such as a sponsored review), we disclose this clearly at the top of the relevant page.
  • No pay-to-rank. Companies cannot pay to improve their rankings or ratings on RankerToolAI.

Which Tools Have Affiliate Programs?

Not all tools we review have affiliate programs. We review tools based on their merit and relevance to our readers, regardless of whether an affiliate program exists. For tools without affiliate programs (such as Midjourney and ChatGPT), we still provide comprehensive reviews because they are important to our readers.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, RankerToolAI discloses its affiliate relationships on this page and on every page where affiliate links appear.

We comply with the FTC's 16 CFR Part 255: "Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising."

Price Accuracy

Pricing information on our site is updated regularly, but prices can change without notice. Always verify current pricing on the vendor's official website before making a purchase decision.
