Is Surfer SEO Worth It?

Discovery at $49/mo is a reasonable entry point for solo bloggers who just need Content Editor scoring on a modest volume of articles. Worth upgrading to Pro or above if you care about how your content performs in AI answer engines (ChatGPT, AI Overviews) — that tracking is exclusive to the higher tiers. If you only need traditional keyword-based content scoring on a tight budget, Frase's lower-cost plans are worth comparing first.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Surfer SEO have a free trial?

Surfer SEO's pricing page no longer advertises a fixed-length free trial — it offers a "Start for Free" signup flow instead. Confirm current trial terms directly on Surfer's site before entering payment details.

What are Surfer SEO's plans and prices?

As of 2026, Surfer SEO offers five annual-billed plans: Discovery ($49/mo, 120 documents), Standard ($99/mo, 360 documents), Pro ($182/mo, 360 documents plus AI prompt tracking), Peace of Mind ($299/mo, unlimited documents plus API access), and Enterprise (custom pricing).

What is AI prompt tracking on Surfer SEO?

Higher-tier plans track how your content performs in AI answer engines like ChatGPT, not just traditional Google rankings. Pro tracks 50 prompts with daily refresh; Peace of Mind tracks 100 prompts with daily refresh across multiple AI models.

Is Surfer SEO worth it compared to Clearscope or Frase?

Surfer offers the best balance of accuracy and price for most content teams. Clearscope is more accurate but costs nearly double; Frase is cheaper but less precise. For solo bloggers on a budget, Frase's lower-cost plans are worth testing first.
