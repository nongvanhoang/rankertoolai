Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a NordPass coupon code in 2026?

There's no separate public coupon code needed — the real discount is choosing the 2-year Premium plan ($1.38/mo) over monthly or 1-year billing, which is roughly 65% cheaper per month than paying month-to-month.

Does NordPass have a free plan?

Yes. NordPass's free plan includes unlimited password storage and a password generator, but restricts you to using one device at a time.

How much does NordPass Premium cost?

NordPass Premium costs $1.99/month on the 1-year plan or $1.38/month on the 2-year plan (billed upfront). Family plans (up to 6 users) start at $3.69/month on 1-year or $2.58/month on 2-year billing.

Is NordPass's 2-year plan a good deal compared to competitors?

Yes — at $1.38/month, it undercuts Bitwarden's roughly $1.65/month Premium tier and 1Password's roughly $3.90/month individual plan, while still including unlimited devices, breach monitoring, and secure sharing. The tradeoff versus Bitwarden is that NordPass's code isn't open source and its free plan restricts you to one device, so the price advantage comes with those two caveats attached.
