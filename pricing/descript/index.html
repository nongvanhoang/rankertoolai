Descript Pricing — FAQs

How much does Descript cost per month?

Free includes 1 hour of transcription/month at $0. Hobbyist costs $24/month, Creator costs $40/month, and Business costs $80/month. Annual billing saves 25% on all paid tiers.

Does Descript have a free trial?

Descript's free plan doesn't expire — it includes 1 hour of transcription/month permanently, with a watermark on exports, so you can evaluate the editor before paying.

What is the cheapest paid Descript plan?

Hobbyist at $24/month (less with annual billing) is the cheapest paid plan. It removes the watermark and unlocks Overdub voice cloning and screen recording.

Can I switch Descript plans?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your Descript plan at any time from your account settings.
