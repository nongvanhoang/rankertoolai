AirOps Pricing — FAQs

How much does AirOps cost per month?

Free covers 1,000 tasks/month at $0. Solo runs roughly $199/month for ~20,000 tasks. Pro runs roughly $1,999/month for ~75,000 tasks. Enterprise pricing is custom.

Does AirOps offer a free trial?

Yes — a 14-day trial of Solo/Pro features with no credit card required, on top of the permanently free 1,000-task plan.

Does AirOps charge overage fees?

Yes. Once you exceed your plan's task allowance, AirOps bills roughly $9 per 1,000 tasks on Solo and $6 per 1,000 tasks on Pro, based on third-party reporting. Confirm current rates with AirOps directly.

Can I switch AirOps plans?

Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Solo or Pro at any time as your task volume grows.
