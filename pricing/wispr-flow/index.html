Wispr Flow Pricing — FAQs

How much does Wispr Flow cost per month?

Basic is free with 2,000 words/week on desktop. Pro costs $12/month billed annually ($15/month monthly). Team costs $10/user/month annually with a 3-seat minimum.

Does Wispr Flow offer a free trial?

Yes — every new account gets 14 days of Pro free with no credit card required. After the trial, you keep the free Basic plan or upgrade.

Is Wispr Flow cheaper than Superwhisper?

Superwhisper is slightly cheaper at $9.99/month but only works on Mac. Wispr Flow's $12/month covers Mac, Windows, iOS, and Android.

Can I switch between Wispr Flow plans?

Yes. You can upgrade from Basic to Pro at any time, and downgrade back to Basic at your next billing cycle.

Is there a student discount for Wispr Flow?

Yes. Verified students get 3 months of Pro free, then 50% off the standard price afterward through Wispr Flow's Flow Discounts program.
