Mangools Pricing — FAQs

How much does Mangools cost per month?

Mangools costs $29/month (Basic), $44/month (Premium), or $89/month (Agency) on monthly billing. Annual billing saves 34–35%: Basic drops to $19/month, Premium to $29/month, Agency to $59/month.

Is KWFinder the same as Mangools?

KWFinder is one of 5 tools inside the Mangools suite. When you subscribe to Mangools, you get KWFinder (keyword research), SERPWatcher (rank tracking), SERPChecker (SERP analysis), LinkMiner (backlinks), and SiteProfiler (domain authority) — all in one subscription.

Does Mangools have a free trial?

Yes — Mangools offers a 10-day free trial with full access to all 5 tools. No credit card is required. You get limited lookups during the trial (same as the Basic plan limits).

Is Mangools worth it for bloggers?

Yes. Mangools Basic at $29/month is one of the best-value SEO tools for bloggers. KWFinder is particularly good at finding low-competition keywords that smaller sites can actually rank for. For the price of a Netflix subscription, you get a complete SEO toolkit.

Can I cancel Mangools anytime?

Yes. Mangools is billed monthly (or annually) with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.
