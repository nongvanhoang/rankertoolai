ElevenLabs Pricing — FAQ

Is ElevenLabs free?

Yes — ElevenLabs has a permanent free plan with 10,000 characters/month (approximately 7 minutes of audio), access to 3 custom voices, and 10+ pre-built voices. No credit card required. The free plan doesn't include a commercial license or voice cloning.

What is the cheapest ElevenLabs plan with voice cloning?

Instant Voice Cloning is available from the Creator plan at $22/month. This lets you clone any voice from a 1-minute audio sample. It's the minimum plan to access this feature.

How many characters is 1 minute of audio?

Approximately 1,400–1,500 characters equals 1 minute of audio speech at a normal reading pace. So 10,000 characters ≈ 7 minutes, 100,000 characters ≈ 70 minutes.

Does ElevenLabs offer annual discounts?

Yes — ElevenLabs offers approximately 20% off when you pay annually. The Creator plan drops from $22/month to about $18/month when billed yearly.

Can I use ElevenLabs commercially on the free plan?

No — the free plan is for personal and evaluation use only. You need at least the Starter plan ($5/month) to get a commercial license for content monetization.

Can I cancel ElevenLabs anytime?

Yes. ElevenLabs is month-to-month with no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings and retain access until your billing period ends.
