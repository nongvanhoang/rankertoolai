iubenda Pricing — FAQs

How much does iubenda cost?

iubenda's free plan covers sites under 1,000 pageviews/month at $0. Essentials starts at $6.99/site/month ($5.99 billed annually). Advanced runs $24.99/site/month. Ultimate is custom-priced for unlimited clauses and features.

Does iubenda charge per site or per account?

Per site. Managing multiple domains means paying the plan rate for each one individually, which matters most for agencies managing several client sites.

What happens if I exceed my pageview limit?

Overage is billed at $0.06 per 1,000 pageviews beyond your plan's included quota, rather than an automatic forced upgrade.

Is iubenda worth it?

iubenda is worth it for small-to-mid-sized businesses that want a bundled Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, and cookie consent management in one subscription. Solo site owners needing only a basic cookie banner may find a cheaper single-purpose tool sufficient instead.

Can I cancel iubenda at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from account settings, and iubenda backs paid plans with a 14-day money-back guarantee if you cancel shortly after upgrading. There's no long-term contract lock-in beyond the billing cycle you've already paid for.
