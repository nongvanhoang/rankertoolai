beehiiv Pricing — FAQs

How much does beehiiv cost per month?

Launch is free forever up to 2,500 subscribers. Scale starts around $49/month ($43/month billed annually) and scales with subscriber count up to roughly $329/month at 100,000 subscribers. Max starts around $109/month ($96/month annually).

Does beehiiv offer a free trial?

Yes — new accounts get a 14-day free trial of Scale or Max features with no credit card required. Through RankerToolAI's link, upgrading during the trial also gets you 20% off your first 3 months.

Is beehiiv cheaper than Substack?

It depends on your paid subscription revenue. Below roughly $490/month, Substack's 10% cut costs less than beehiiv's flat monthly fee. Above that, beehiiv's flat fee — which doesn't scale with revenue — becomes the cheaper option.

Can I switch plans on beehiiv?

Yes. You can upgrade from Launch to Scale or Max at any time, and downgrade at your next billing cycle. Subscriber data, website, and archive carry over automatically.

Does beehiiv have a money-back guarantee?

beehiiv doesn't advertise a formal money-back guarantee, but the 14-day free trial of paid features means you can test Scale or Max risk-free before paying anything.
