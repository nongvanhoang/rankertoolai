NordPass Pricing — FAQs

How much does NordPass cost?

NordPass's free plan is $0 (1 device at a time). Premium costs $1.99/month on the 1-year plan or $1.38/month on the 2-year plan. Family (up to 6 users) starts at $3.69/month (1-year) or $2.58/month (2-year).

Does NordPass have a free trial?

NordPass's free plan doesn't expire — it includes unlimited password storage on one device, permanently, so you can evaluate the app before paying.

What is the cheapest way to get NordPass Premium?

The 2-year Premium plan at $1.38/month is the cheapest per-month rate NordPass offers, though it requires paying the full 2-year term upfront.

Can I switch NordPass plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Premium, or switch between Premium and Family, at any time from your NordPass account settings.

Does NordPass offer a refund if I don't like it?

NordPass offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on new Premium and Family subscriptions, so you can request a full refund if it doesn't work out within the first month — a lower-risk way to commit to the cheaper 2-year term without being fully locked in immediately.
