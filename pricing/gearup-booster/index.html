GearUP Booster Pricing — FAQs

How much does GearUP Booster cost?

Pricing varies by region and plan: standard membership typically runs $4.49-9.99/month, a 3-month plan runs about $29.99, and annual plans run roughly $79.99-99/year. Router Membership is $9.99 and Mobile Membership is $6.99, both separate add-ons.

Is there a free version of GearUP Booster?

There's a 3-day free trial rather than an ongoing free plan, giving new users enough time to test real ping/packet-loss improvement before subscribing.

Does GearUP Booster sell hardware?

Yes. HYPEREV Gaming Routers start around $39.99, and HYPEREV console hardware runs about $55.99 as a one-time purchase, separate from the software membership plans.

Is GearUP Booster worth it?

It's worth it for players whose default ISP route to game servers is inefficient, especially households wanting the router-level Membership to boost consoles and multiple devices at once. Players already on a direct, well-peered connection may see little to no benefit — test during the 3-day trial before committing.

Does the 3-day trial require a credit card?

Yes, GearUP Booster's free trial requires a payment method on file and automatically converts to a paid subscription if not cancelled before the trial ends — set a reminder to cancel if you don't see a measurable ping improvement during your test, since there's no standard money-back guarantee once billing starts.
