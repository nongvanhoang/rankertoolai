Best AI Marketing Tools 2026

We tested 12+ AI marketing platforms — ad creative generators, email personalization engines, and campaign automation tools. Here's what actually drives results.

✓ Updated June 2026

1
HubSpot AI
Best AI marketing suite for growing teams
Price: Free — $800+/mo
Free plan: Yes
Best for: Teams already using HubSpot

HubSpot AI is now deeply embedded across its Marketing, CRM, and Sales Hubs — generating email copy, blog drafts, social captions, and CTAs directly in context. The AI works with your existing contact data for personalization. If you're already in the HubSpot ecosystem, the AI features are a significant productivity multiplier at no extra cost.

AdCreative.ai
Best AI ad creative generator
Price: $21/mo — $149/mo
Free plan: No (7-day trial)
Best for: Performance marketers, media buyers

AdCreative.ai generates conversion-optimized ad creatives for Google, Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn from your brand assets. It scores each creative before publishing, predicting CTR. Teams using it report 2–4x faster creative production. Best for performance marketers running paid social at scale.

Jasper
Best AI writer for marketing copy
Price: $39/mo
Free plan: No (7-day trial)
Best for: Content-heavy marketing teams

Jasper's AI Campaigns feature generates complete multi-channel marketing campaigns (email, social, blog, ads) from a single brief. Brand Voice ensures consistency across all outputs. Copy.ai
Best free AI marketing tool with workflow automation
Price: Free — $36/mo
Free plan: Yes
Best for: Teams automating marketing workflows

Copy.ai's Workflows automate multi-step marketing tasks — from competitor research to first draft to SEO optimization — without human intervention. 90+ marketing templates cover every copy format. The free plan is generous enough for small teams. Best value for teams wanting workflow automation without the HubSpot price tag. Read our full Copy.ai review →

Seventh Sense
Best AI email send-time optimization
Price: $64/mo (HubSpot) / $360/mo (Marketo)
Free plan: No
Best for: Email-heavy marketing teams

Seventh Sense uses AI to determine the optimal send time for every individual contact in your email list based on their past engagement behavior. Teams using it report 20–35% improvements in open rates. It integrates exclusively with HubSpot and Marketo — not a standalone tool, but an exceptional add-on for email marketers.

Phrasee
Best AI for email subject line optimization
Price: Custom (enterprise)
Free plan: No
Best for: Enterprise email marketers

Phrasee uses AI trained on brand language to generate and optimize email subject lines, push notifications, and social copy. It predicts performance before sending and learns from results to improve over time. Enterprise pricing makes it suitable for large retailers and financial services companies sending millions of emails.
