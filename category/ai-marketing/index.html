How to Choose the Best AI Marketing Tool

The right AI marketing tool depends on where your biggest bottleneck sits. If you're a team already using HubSpot, the AI features built into the platform are a no-brainer — zero extra cost, no context switching, and the AI works with your existing contact and campaign data. If your primary pain point is ad creative production, AdCreative.ai is purpose-built for performance marketers who need high-volume, conversion-optimized creative at speed. For content-heavy marketing teams, Jasper's Brand Voice and multi-channel Campaigns feature set is the most complete available.

Budget matters significantly in this category. HubSpot AI is included in existing HubSpot plans — making it cost $0 for teams already subscribed. Copy.ai has a genuinely useful free plan. AdCreative.ai and Jasper require paid plans starting at $21/month and $59/month respectively. Enterprise tools like Phrasee and Seventh Sense are priced for large-scale email programs with thousands of contacts.

The most important question to ask before purchasing any AI marketing tool is: what's your primary use case? Email optimization, ad creative, content writing, and social media automation are different problems requiring different tools. Avoid the temptation to buy a suite that "does everything" — the best outcomes come from purpose-built tools matched to your highest-volume, highest-impact marketing activities.

What to Look for in AI Marketing Tools

  • CRM Integration: Tools that plug into your existing CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce) use your actual customer data for personalization, producing better results than generic AI outputs.
  • Brand Voice Controls: Jasper and Copy.ai both offer brand voice training so AI outputs stay on-brand. Critical for agencies and teams with strict brand standards.
  • Output Volume vs. Quality: High-volume tools (AdCreative.ai, Copy.ai) optimize for speed and scale. Enterprise tools (Jasper, Phrasee) optimize for quality and brand consistency.
  • Performance Prediction: AdCreative.ai scores creatives before publishing. Phrasee predicts open rates. These predictive features meaningfully improve ROI versus guessing.
  • Workflow Automation: Copy.ai Workflows and Jasper Campaigns automate multi-step content production. This is where the biggest productivity gains live for content-heavy teams.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What AI tools do marketers actually use?

In 2026, the most widely adopted AI marketing tools are ChatGPT (for general copy assistance), Jasper (for structured marketing content workflows), and HubSpot AI (for teams on the HubSpot platform). AdCreative.ai is the dominant choice for performance marketers running paid social at scale. Surfer SEO is widely used by content teams for SEO-optimized article production.

Q: How does AI help with marketing?

AI accelerates marketing across three key areas: content creation (drafting blogs, emails, ads, and social posts faster), personalization (tailoring messages to individual contacts using CRM data), and optimization (predicting creative performance before publishing, improving send times, and A/B testing at scale). Teams using AI marketing tools consistently report 40–70% reductions in content production time.

Q: What is the best free AI marketing tool?

Copy.ai offers the best free AI marketing tier — unlimited credits for most templates including email, social posts, and product descriptions. ChatGPT's free tier is also widely used for one-off marketing copy. HubSpot's free CRM includes limited AI features. For teams with a budget of $0, the combination of Copy.ai (free) and ChatGPT (free) covers the majority of everyday marketing writing tasks.

Q: Is Jasper worth it for small marketing teams?

For small teams producing low content volume (fewer than 10 pieces per week), Jasper's $59/month price tag is hard to justify — ChatGPT Plus at $20/month covers most of the same capabilities. Jasper's value becomes clear for teams producing 20+ content pieces weekly who need brand voice enforcement and structured campaign workflows. At that scale, the time savings justify the cost.
