Our Verdict

AdCreative.ai's core idea is genuinely useful: its Creative Scoring AI predicts ad performance with 90%+ claimed accuracy by analyzing components (logos, CTAs, product placement, text hierarchy) and visual attention patterns, so you can prioritize which designs to actually run before spending ad budget testing all of them. Generation is fast — 20+ static ad variants in under 5 minutes for Facebook, Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn. The catch is the pricing structure: the $39/month Starter plan looks affordable until you realize video ad generation is completely locked behind the $249/month Professional plan, credits don't roll over between months, and post-generation customization is more limited than a dedicated design tool. For teams running high-volume static image ads, it earns its price. For anyone who needs video on a budget, look elsewhere or budget for Professional from day one.

Key Features Tested

Creative Scoring AI

AdCreative.ai's standout feature combines Component Analysis AI (evaluating logos, CTAs, product placement, and text hierarchy) with Saliency AI (predicting where a viewer's eyes go first using eye-tracking-trained models), producing a single score that claims 90%+ accuracy in predicting how an ad will perform. In our testing, this made it easy to quickly rank a batch of generated variants and prioritize which ones to actually spend budget testing, rather than running all 20+ variants blind.

Generation Speed & Formats

AdCreative.ai produced 20+ static ad variants for a single product in under 5 minutes during our testing — comparable to what would take a designer hours to mock up manually. It covers static image ads across Facebook, Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn, plus social media posts and enhanced product photography. Video ads, including UGC-style content, are supported but require the Professional plan.

Ad Platform Integrations

Direct integration with Facebook and Google Ads lets you push generated creatives into active campaigns without a separate export/upload step, which meaningfully speeds up the test-and-iterate cycle for performance marketing teams already running paid social and search.

Credits & Customization

Every plan is credit-based (1 credit per downloaded creative), and credits reset each billing cycle without rolling over — meaning a slow month wastes unused capacity. Post-generation editing is more limited than a dedicated tool like Canva or Photoshop; AdCreative.ai is built for fast bulk generation and scoring, not fine-grained manual design control.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AdCreative.ai?

AdCreative.ai is an AI-powered advertising platform, now owned by Appier, that generates conversion-optimized ad creatives at scale. Its Creative Scoring AI combines Component Analysis AI and Saliency AI to score ad designs before you spend money running them.

Does AdCreative.ai have a free plan?

No permanent free plan — AdCreative.ai offers a 7-day free trial with 10 credits, but you must subscribe to a paid plan once the trial expires or credits run out.

How much does AdCreative.ai cost?

Starter plans begin at $39/month for 10 credits. Professional is $249/month for 100 credits. Professional Ultimate includes 500 credits and supports up to 10 brands. Annual billing gives roughly a 40% discount. Video ad generation requires Professional or higher.

Is AdCreative.ai worth it?

For teams running paid social/search ads at meaningful volume, yes — the Creative Scoring AI genuinely helps prioritize which designs to test, and generating 20+ variants in under 5 minutes saves real design time. It's a weaker fit for anyone who mainly needs video ads on a budget, since video is locked behind the $249/month tier.

Does AdCreative.ai generate video ads?

Yes, including UGC-style video content, but only on the Professional plan ($249/month) or higher — video features are completely locked on the $39/month Starter plan.
