What is Midjourney?

Midjourney is an independent AI lab and the creator of the Midjourney image generation model. Founded in 2021, it became one of the first AI image generators to go mainstream. The tool is primarily accessed through a Discord server, though a web interface at midjourney.com is now in open beta.

Midjourney v6.1 (current as of 2026) produces images of extraordinary detail, with realistic skin textures, natural lighting, and cinematic composition that still leads the industry. Unlike OpenAI or Google, Midjourney is privately held and does not publish papers or model details — making it something of a "black box" compared to competitors.

Image Quality Testing: 500+ Generations

We ran 500 image prompts across photorealism, artistic styles, portraits, landscapes, architecture, and products. Here's what we found:

Photorealism: 9.3/10

Midjourney v6.1's photorealistic portrait generation is best-in-class. Skin textures, eye detail, and hair rendering are noticeably more realistic than DALL-E 3 or Firefly. In 95% of our tests, images were indistinguishable from stock photography at a glance.

Artistic Styles: 9.8/10

From oil painting to cyberpunk digital art to watercolor, Midjourney's style adherence and quality is unmatched. The --sref (style reference) parameter allows you to maintain consistent aesthetics across multiple generations — invaluable for brand consistency.

Prompt Accuracy: 8.4/10

This is Midjourney's weakest area compared to DALL-E 3. Complex prompts (specific object counts, precise spatial relationships, text in images) are often interpreted loosely. Midjourney "interprets" prompts artistically rather than literally — which produces beautiful results but not always what you asked for.

Text in Images: 5.5/10

Midjourney still struggles significantly with rendering readable text in images. For logos, posters, or any design requiring typography, use Ideogram or DALL-E 3 instead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Midjourney free in 2026?

No. Midjourney removed its free trial in 2024. The cheapest plan is Basic at $10/month for approximately 200 fast-generated images. Free alternatives include Adobe Firefly (25 credits/month) and Bing Image Creator (DALL-E 3, weekly boosts).

Is Midjourney v6 better than v5?

Yes, significantly. V6.1 produces dramatically more photorealistic images, better handles complex prompts, and has improved text rendering (though still weak). The default "aesthetic" has also shifted — V6 produces more natural-looking images vs V5's more stylized look.

Can I use Midjourney images commercially?

On paid plans, yes — you have rights to use generated images commercially. However, Midjourney's training data includes copyrighted images without licensing, which creates unresolved legal risk for some commercial applications. For full commercial safety, use Adobe Firefly (trained on licensed content).

How do I use Midjourney without Discord?

Midjourney.com now has a web interface in open beta. Go to midjourney.com, sign in with your Discord account, and use the image creation tools directly in your browser. The web interface is still slightly behind the Discord workflow in features, but it's fully functional for most tasks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Midjourney cost?

Midjourney offers four plans: Basic ($10/month, ~200 images), Standard ($30/month, unlimited relaxed + 15hr fast), Pro ($60/month, unlimited relaxed + 30hr fast + stealth mode), and Mega ($120/month, 60hr fast). Annual plans are 20% cheaper. The Standard plan is the most popular for regular users. There's no free trial currently — you must subscribe to generate images.

What is Midjourney best for?

Midjourney excels at: artistic and creative concept imagery, photorealistic portraits, editorial and magazine-style visuals, fantasy and sci-fi artwork, product visualization, architectural concepts, and any creative work where aesthetic sophistication matters. It's less suitable for: text-in-images (it often renders text incorrectly), technical diagrams, or images requiring very literal prompt adherence.

Is Midjourney better than DALL-E 3?

Midjourney produces higher overall artistic quality and aesthetic sophistication — particularly for creative and editorial work. DALL-E 3 is better for text accuracy in images, conversational refinement (you can ask ChatGPT to modify the image through chat), and prompt literalness. DALL-E 3 is included with ChatGPT Plus ($20/month), making it free at the margin for existing subscribers. Most professional image creators prefer Midjourney for quality.

How do I use Midjourney?

Midjourney operates through Discord. Join the Midjourney Discord server, find a #newbies channel, and use the /imagine command followed by your prompt. The bot generates 4 image variations. Click U1-U4 to upscale a specific image, or V1-V4 to generate variations of it. Key parameters: --ar for aspect ratio (--ar 16:9), --stylize for artistic interpretation strength (--stylize 0-1000), --chaos for variation (--chaos 0-100).
