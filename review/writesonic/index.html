What Is Writesonic?

Writesonic launched in 2020 as an AI writing platform (Chatsonic, Article Writer). As of 2026, the company has fully repositioned as an AI Search Growth Engine — the older content-writing product line is no longer what's marketed or sold on writesonic.com.

The new product tracks how a brand is represented across AI-generated answers — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other AI search platforms — by monitoring an internal dataset of 2 billion+ AI conversations. It then prioritizes visibility gaps and executes fixes (content and technical) through AI agents, and measures the resulting lift in traffic and revenue. The company calls this workflow the "AI Search Loop."

This puts Writesonic in a newer product category sometimes called "AEO" (Answer Engine Optimization) or "GEO" (Generative Engine Optimization) — alongside tools like Profound and Otterly — rather than competing with AI writing tools like Jasper or Copy.ai.

Writesonic Key Features (2026)

1. AI Visibility Tracking

Monitors how often and how favorably a brand is mentioned across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other AI platforms, drawing on a dataset described as 2 billion+ AI conversations. This is the core dashboard most customers use daily.

2. Action Center

Prioritizes visibility gaps by potential impact and suggests specific fixes — content updates, schema/technical changes, or outreach — rather than just reporting a score.

3. Automated Content Optimization

Adjusts existing content to be more "citation-worthy" for AI answer engines, based on patterns in what AI models tend to cite.

4. Citation Outreach

Tools for pursuing citations and mentions on high-authority third-party sites (Reddit, YouTube, and similar), since AI answer engines frequently draw from these sources.

5. Technical Audits

Checks robots.txt and schema markup for AI-crawler accessibility — a technical layer most traditional SEO tools don't cover.

6. ROI Measurement

Tracks the traffic and revenue lift from executed fixes at 14 and 28 days post-fix.

Note: we have not yet run hands-on tests of this new product version — the above is based on Writesonic's own public product materials, not our independent testing. We'll update this section with first-hand results.
