5 Tools Inside Mangools — Tested

KWFinder — Keyword Research

KWFinder is the flagship tool and the reason most people subscribe to Mangools. Enter any keyword and you get search volume, trend data, CPC, and a Keyword Difficulty (KD) score from 0–100. In our testing, KWFinder's KD scores were the most actionable we found at this price point — far less prone to over-inflating difficulty on low-competition terms than Semrush. The related keyword suggestions and SERP preview side-by-side view make it easy to evaluate search intent and competition without leaving the page.

SERPChecker — SERP Analysis

SERPChecker breaks down the top 20 results for any keyword, showing DA, PA, Citation Flow, Trust Flow, Facebook shares, and more for each ranking page. The color-coded weakness indicators highlight which positions have low-authority pages you can realistically outrank. In our tests against live SERPs, the data matched Moz and Majestic metrics within 5–10% variance.

SERPWatcher — Rank Tracking

SERPWatcher tracks keyword positions daily and calculates a "Dominance Index" — a single score that reflects both your rankings and the search volumes of the tracked keywords. It's a smart metric that shows ranking progress in business terms rather than just average position. The entry-level plan tracks up to 200 keywords, which is sufficient for most small sites.

LinkMiner — Backlink Analysis

LinkMiner shows referring domains, anchor text, link strength, and found/lost backlinks over time. The database is powered by Majestic data, which is solid for trust and citation flow metrics but smaller than Ahrefs for raw backlink count. For monitoring your own profile or quick competitor checks, it's adequate. For serious link building campaigns, you'd want a dedicated tool.

SiteProfiler — Domain Overview

SiteProfiler gives a domain-level overview: authority score, backlink profile, top content, competitor domains, and keyword overlap. It's useful for quick competitive research and vetting potential guest post targets. It won't replace a full competitive intelligence suite, but the data quality is reliable for its intended use.

Best Mangools Alternatives

Mangools isn't for everyone. Here are the top alternatives depending on your needs:

  • SE Ranking — Full SEO suite with deeper site audit and more keywords tracked. From $65/mo. Better for agencies managing multiple sites.
  • Semrush — Enterprise-grade competitive intelligence and the largest keyword database. From $139/mo.
