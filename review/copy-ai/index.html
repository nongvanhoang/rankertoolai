Our Verdict

Copy.ai in 2026 is not the tool most people searching this review remember. The company has repositioned as a "GTM AI" platform — automating sales, marketing, and operations workflows for revenue teams, marketed around eliminating "GTM bloat" — with 17 million users cited across Fortune 500 companies. The old free plan, 90+ templates, and $36/month Starter tier no longer exist in that form. If you came here for a budget AI copywriting tool, this isn't it anymore; see Jasper instead. If you're evaluating GTM/revenue-operations automation for a team, Copy.ai is worth a look, but pricing now starts at $29/month for a basic tier and scales into four figures for team plans.

What Copy.ai Does Now

1. Chat (AI Assistant)

A chat interface with access to OpenAI, Anthropic, and Gemini models, aimed at day-to-day GTM tasks rather than long-form content drafting specifically.

2. Workflow Credits & Automation

Paid team plans (Growth and above) include a monthly pool of workflow credits for automating multi-step GTM processes — the spiritual successor to the old Workflows feature, but now positioned around sales/marketing/ops automation broadly rather than content pipelines specifically.

3. Seat-Based Team Plans

Plans now scale by seat count (5 → 75 → 150 → 200+), reflecting a shift toward outfitting entire revenue teams rather than individual writers or small marketing teams.

Note: we have not yet run hands-on tests of this new product version — the above is based on Copy.ai's own public product materials, not independent testing. We'll update this section with first-hand results.

Who Should Use Copy.ai Now?

Copy.ai may be the right choice if: you're a marketing, sales, or revenue-operations team looking to automate GTM workflows at scale, and you have budget for a $29-3,000+/month team tool. The seat-based pricing and workflow-credit system suggest it's built for coordinated team deployments, not individual use.

Copy.ai is not the right choice if: you're a solo writer, blogger, or small business looking for an affordable AI copywriting tool — that product no longer exists here. Look at Jasper, or a general-purpose AI chat tool, instead.

Our bottom line: this is a legitimate-looking pivot into a real, growing category (GTM/revenue automation), but it's a fundamentally different product than the one this review originally covered. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Copy.ai still an AI copywriting tool?

No — Copy.ai has repositioned as a "GTM AI" platform, focused on automating sales, marketing, and operations workflows for teams, rather than generating marketing copy for individual writers.

Does Copy.ai still have a free plan?

No. The old free plan with unlimited words for 1 user no longer applies. The current cheapest plan is Chat at $29/month ($24/month billed annually) for 5 seats.

How does Copy.ai compare to Jasper now?

They now serve different purposes. Jasper remains focused on content generation and brand voice for marketing copy. Copy.ai has moved into broader GTM workflow automation for revenue teams — a different, more expensive category aimed at larger organizations.

How much does Copy.ai cost now?

Chat is $29/month (5 seats). Growth is $1,000/month (75 seats). Expansion is $2,000/month (150 seats). Scale is $3,000/month (200 seats). Enterprise is custom-priced.
