Anyword Features — Tested

Predictive Performance Score

Every piece of copy Anyword generates gets a 0–100 Performance Score predicting how well it will convert, based on a model trained on real ad performance data across industries. In our testing, this wasn't just a gimmick — variants scoring above 80 had a measurably higher actual click-through rate than lower-scored variants in a controlled split test.

Ad Copy Generation

Anyword generates dozens of ad copy variants per request, tailored to platform (Meta, Google, LinkedIn) and target audience/tone. The volume and relevance of variants beat every generalist AI writer we tested for this specific use case.

Brand Voice

You can train a custom Brand Voice profile from existing copy, and Anyword will keep new generations consistent with that tone across every team member's output — useful for agencies managing copy for multiple brands or in-house teams enforcing brand guidelines.

Landing Page & Email Copy

Beyond ads, Anyword handles landing page headlines, body copy, and email subject lines with the same Performance Score attached, letting you A/B test predicted winners before committing ad spend to a live test.

Ad Platform Integrations

Direct integrations with Meta Ads Manager and Google Ads let you push generated copy straight into live campaigns and pull back performance data to refine future generations — closing the loop between prediction and reality.

Best Anyword Alternatives

Anyword isn't for everyone. Here are the top alternatives depending on your needs:

  • Jasper — Better for long-form blog content and brand-wide content workflows. No predictive performance scoring.
  • Copy.ai — Cheaper, broader template library, weaker on performance prediction.
  • Writesonic — Strong all-rounder with SEO features built in, better value for mixed content needs.
  • Persado — Enterprise-grade predictive copy at a much higher price point — for large brands only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Anyword worth it?

Anyword is worth it specifically for performance marketers running paid ad campaigns, where its Predictive Performance Score helps prioritize which copy variants to test first. For long-form content teams, Jasper or Writesonic are better value.

Does Anyword have a free trial?

Yes, Anyword offers a 7-day free trial on its Starter plan with no major feature restrictions, letting you fully test the Performance Score and ad copy generation before paying.

How much does Anyword cost?

Anyword's Starter plan is $39/month for a single user. The Data-Driven plan for teams typically runs around $199/month, and Business (custom, unlimited generation, API access) requires a custom quote.

What is Anyword's Performance Score?

It's a 0–100 score predicting how well a piece of generated copy will convert, based on a model trained on real ad performance data. In our testing, copy scoring 80+ measurably outperformed lower-scored variants in click-through rate.

Anyword vs Jasper — which is better?

Anyword wins for ad copy and performance marketing thanks to its predictive scoring; Jasper wins for long-form blog content and brand-wide content workflows. Many marketing teams use both for different purposes.

Is Anyword good for agencies?

Yes — the Business tier supports unlimited team members and brand voice profiles per client, and API access allows agencies to build copy generation into their own internal tools.
