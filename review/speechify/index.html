Our Verdict

Speechify is the best app in its category for turning any text — articles, PDFs, emails, even photos of physical book pages — into audio you can listen to hands-free. The premium AI voices sound natural even at 2–3x speed, which is the real use case: most users aren't listening at 1x, they're using Speechify to consume more content faster. It's a different tool than ElevenLabs or Play.ht, which are built for producing voiceover content for others to hear — Speechify is built for you to listen. If you read a lot of long-form content, PDFs, or research papers and want to reclaim time, it's an easy recommendation. If you need to generate voiceovers for videos or products, look at ElevenLabs instead.

Speechify Features — Tested

Premium AI Voices at High Speed

Speechify's core use case is listening at 2x, 3x, or faster — not 1x narration. In our testing, the premium voices held up remarkably well at 2.5x speed with minimal robotic artifacts, which is the feature that matters most for power users trying to get through a backlog of articles or research papers.

OCR Document & Book Scanning

Point your phone camera at a physical book page, and Speechify's OCR will read it aloud. We tested this against several printed pages and accuracy was high even with mixed fonts — a feature dedicated TTS competitors generally don't offer at this quality.

Browser Extension

The Chrome extension turns any webpage, including most PDFs opened in-browser, into audio with one click. This is the feature most users interact with daily — it works on news articles, blog posts, and most text-heavy pages without configuration.

Cross-Device Sync

Speechify syncs your listening library and playback position across iOS, Android, Mac, and Windows, so you can start an article on desktop and continue listening on your phone during a commute.

Speechify Premium+

The higher Premium+ tier adds custom AI voice cloning and additional premium voices, positioning it slightly closer to a production tool — though ElevenLabs remains the stronger choice if voice cloning for content production (not personal listening) is your primary goal.

Best Speechify Alternatives

Speechify isn't for everyone. Here are the top alternatives depending on your needs:

  • ElevenLabs — Best choice if you need to produce voiceovers for videos or products rather than personal listening.
  • Natural Reader — Cheaper alternative with solid OCR, fewer premium voice options.
  • Voice Dream Reader — iOS-focused reader app with strong accessibility features for dyslexia support.
  • Play.ht — Better for generating long-form narrated audio content (audiobooks, podcasts) at scale.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Speechify worth it?

Speechify is worth it for anyone who reads a lot of long-form content, PDFs, or research and wants to consume it faster via audio. At roughly $11.58/month billed annually, it pays for itself quickly in reclaimed time for students, researchers, and heavy readers.

Does Speechify have a free version?

Yes, Speechify offers a free version with limited voice selection and slower maximum playback speed. The Premium plan unlocks all voices and speeds up to 4.5x.

How much does Speechify cost?

Speechify Premium costs roughly $139/year (about $11.58/month) when billed annually, or a higher month-to-month rate. Premium+ with voice cloning costs more, typically around $24/month.

Speechify vs ElevenLabs — which should I use?

Use Speechify if you want to listen to text you're consuming (articles, PDFs, books). Use ElevenLabs if you want to produce voiceover audio for videos, products, or other people to listen to. They solve different problems despite both using AI voices.

Can Speechify read physical books?

Yes — Speechify's OCR camera scanning feature can photograph a physical book page and read it aloud. In our testing, accuracy was strong even on pages with mixed fonts, though very small or stylized text can reduce accuracy.

Does Speechify work on all devices?

Speechify is available on iOS, Android, Mac, Windows, and as a Chrome browser extension, with your library and playback position synced across devices.
