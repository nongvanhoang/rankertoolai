What iubenda Offers

Privacy & Cookie Policy Generator

iubenda's core product generates a Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy tailored to the specific services your site actually uses — analytics, ad networks, embedded video, payment processors — rather than a generic template. Essentials covers up to 20 services in one language; Advanced and above add unlimited custom clauses and every supported language, which matters for any site serving international traffic where a single-language policy doesn't meet local disclosure norms.

Cookie Solution (CMP) — Script Blocking, Not Just a Banner

This is the feature that separates iubenda from a basic policy generator: the Cookie Solution is an actual Consent Management Platform that categorizes cookies (necessary, analytics, marketing) and blocks the non-essential ones from loading until a visitor gives informed consent. A banner that simply displays a notice without blocking scripts does not meet GDPR's standard for valid consent — script-blocking is the functional requirement, and it's what iubenda is built to do.

Automated Site Scanning

Essentials includes monthly automated scans of your site to detect new cookies and tracking scripts; Advanced keeps the same monthly cadence, while Ultimate steps up to hourly scans. This matters because a manually-maintained cookie list silently goes stale the moment a marketing team adds a new tracking pixel — automated scanning is what keeps the declared policy in sync with what's actually running on the site.

Geo-Targeting & IAB TCF Support

Advanced and Ultimate plans add geo-targeted consent rules, serving GDPR's opt-in flow to EU/UK visitors and a CCPA-appropriate opt-out flow to California visitors automatically, rather than applying one blanket banner worldwide. iubenda's Cookie Solution also supports the IAB Transparency & Consent Framework (TCF), which most major ad networks now require to keep serving programmatic ads to EU traffic without a compliance-related revenue hit.

Consent Logging & Ultimate-Tier Extras

Every plan keeps a timestamped, exportable record of what each visitor consented to — useful if a regulator or a partner ever asks for proof. The Ultimate plan adds full branding removal, a mobile SDK for apps, detailed consent analytics, and consent recovery, aimed at larger sites and businesses that need deeper reporting and a fully white-labeled banner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is iubenda worth it?

For any site running analytics, ads, or other non-essential tracking scripts, yes. iubenda combines a Privacy & Cookie Policy generator with an actual script-blocking Cookie Solution (CMP), rather than just a notice banner — which is what GDPR compliance actually requires. Does iubenda have a free plan?

Does iubenda actually block cookies before consent?
