What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant made by Anthropic, an AI safety company founded in 2021 by former OpenAI researchers. The name "Claude" refers to both the AI product and the underlying language models (Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Claude 3 Haiku, etc.).

Anthropic's approach differs from competitors: their "Constitutional AI" training method embeds explicit principles into the model, making Claude follow nuanced instructions while maintaining strong safety properties — without the over-filtering that plagues many AI tools. Claude is used by developers via the API, businesses through Anthropic's B2B products, and individuals through claude.ai.

Performance: How Claude Compares in 2026

Writing Quality — Why Claude Leads

We tested 50 identical writing tasks across Claude Sonnet, GPT-4o, and Gemini Advanced. Claude won on 34/50 tasks according to blind evaluation by our team. The difference is most pronounced in:

  • Tone consistency: Claude maintains a specified voice across long documents without drift
  • Nuanced editing: When given complex editing instructions ("make this more formal but keep the casual examples"), Claude executes them correctly where other models oversimplify
  • Long-form quality: At 2,000+ words, Claude maintains coherence and logical flow better than GPT-4o
  • Instruction adherence: Claude follows multi-constraint prompts ("write in exactly 150 words, no bullets, third person, formal") with higher accuracy

200K Context Window — What It Means

Claude's 200K token context window is its most distinctive technical advantage. To put it in perspective:

Claude 3.5 Sonnet200K tokens(~150,000 words / 600 pages)
GPT-4o128K tokens(~96,000 words / 384 pages)
Gemini 1.5 Pro1M tokens(~750,000 words — theoretical)

In practice, Claude's 200K window means you can paste an entire codebase, a full legal contract, or a research paper and ask questions about it without the context being truncated.

Coding Performance (9.1/10)

Claude 3.5 Sonnet is excellent at coding — particularly at writing clean, well-structured code and explaining code in plain English. It edges out GPT-4o on frontend development tasks and code review, while GPT-4o has a slight advantage on competitive programming problems. Both are dramatically better than older models.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Claude better than ChatGPT for writing?

Yes — in our testing, Claude Sonnet produces better writing quality than GPT-4o for most text tasks, particularly long-form content, nuanced editing, and following complex formatting instructions. On our 50-task blind evaluation, Claude won 68% of writing comparisons.

What is the difference between Claude Sonnet and Haiku?

Claude Sonnet is Anthropic's primary model — the best balance of capability and speed. Claude Haiku is faster and cheaper (for API use) but less capable. Claude Opus is the most powerful model, available via API and sometimes accessible on the Pro plan. For most users, Sonnet is the right choice.

Does Claude save my conversations?

By default, Anthropic stores conversations to improve Claude. On the free and Pro plans, you can adjust this in Privacy settings. Team and Enterprise plans offer stronger data controls, including the option to prevent any training on your data.

Can Claude access the internet?

Yes — web search is available on Claude Pro and higher plans. The free tier currently has limited web access. When search is enabled, Claude can retrieve current information, check facts, and research recent events.

Who is Claude made by?

Anthropic, an AI safety company founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei (CEO), Daniela Amodei (President), and other former OpenAI researchers. Anthropic focuses on AI safety research and building AI systems that are reliable, interpretable, and steerable.

Our Verdict: 9.2/10 — Best for Writing

Claude 3.5 Sonnet is the best AI assistant for writing, document analysis, and complex reasoning in 2026. Its 200K context window, instruction-following quality, and strong free tier make it the top recommendation for writers, researchers, lawyers, and developers who work primarily with text.

The only significant gap: no image generation. If you need images, pair Claude with DALL-E 3 (via ChatGPT) or Midjourney.
