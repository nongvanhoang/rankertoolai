Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit a tool for review?

Yes. Use the contact form and select "Submit a Tool for Review." We review all submissions but cannot guarantee every tool will be reviewed. We prioritize tools with significant user bases or unique capabilities.

Can I pay to get a better review or ranking?

No. We do not accept payment in exchange for reviews, ratings, or rankings. If you'd like to reach our audience, see our partnership inquiry option for legitimate advertising options.

I found an error in a review. What should I do?

Please use the contact form and select "Report an Error." Include the page URL and a description of the error. We take accuracy seriously and will investigate and update within a few business days.

Do you do sponsored content or brand partnerships?

We only consider partnerships that are clearly disclosed to readers and do not influence our editorial ratings. Select "Partnership Inquiry" in the form to start a conversation.
