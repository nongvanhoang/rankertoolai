1. Overview & Goals

Our SEO priority is domain authority and topical authority for "AI tools" queries. We have excellent on-page content — the bottleneck is backlinks. Target: 30 referring domains from DR 40+ sites in 90 days. Focus exclusively on white-hat, sustainable tactics.

Priority pages for link building:

  • /best/ai-writing-tools/ — high commercial value
  • /best/free-ai-tools/ — high search volume
  • /review/chatgpt/ — highest traffic potential
  • /review/claude/ — writing AI comparison queries
  • /blog/chatgpt-vs-claude-for-writing/ — great for journalist outreach

4. Tactic 3: HARO / Journalist Outreach

Process

  • Sign up for Help a Reporter Out (HARO) — free tier, 3 emails/day with journalist queries
  • Monitor #tech, #software, #AI categories for quotes needed
  • Respond within 1 hour of receiving queries — first response wins
  • Lead with one data point from our testing (e.g., "In our 20-task writing test, Claude beat ChatGPT 14/20 times")
  • Keep responses under 150 words — journalists scan fast

Secondary: Twitter/X & LinkedIn Outreach

Engage with AI journalists and bloggers who cover the space: reply to their threads with genuinely useful data from our testing. Build relationship before pitching. Target: 3-5 quality interactions/week with writers at TechCrunch, The Verge, Wired, Bloomberg Technology.

5. Tactic 4: Broken Link Building

Process

  • Use Ahrefs / Semrush to find broken backlinks on competitor review sites (trustpilot.com/review/ai-tools, alternativeto.net)
  • Find pages linking to dead AI tool reviews (tools that have shut down: Lex.page pivoted, some Jasper competitors closed)
  • Reach out to the linking page offering our review as replacement
  • Target 5-10 broken link opportunities per month

6. Tactic 5: Original Research & Data

Original data earns links passively. Planned research assets:

  • "AI Tools Pricing Index 2026" — monthly price tracking across 30 tools, publish as a page that journalists can cite
  • "AI Writing Quality Benchmark" — our 20-task blind test results as a shareable data page with methodology
  • "Free AI Tools Database" — searchable table of 50+ tools with free tier limits

Promote research assets via Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and to newsletters (Ben's Bites, The Rundown AI, TLDR AI).

8. What NOT to Do

  • No link buying or paid link insertions — instant Google penalty risk
  • No PBNs (private blog networks)
  • No reciprocal link exchanges with unrelated sites
  • No spammy directory submissions (DA < 20, keyword-stuffed anchor text)
  • No automated outreach tools — personalized emails only
