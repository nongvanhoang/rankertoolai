The Real Test: 12 Videos Across 3 Weeks

Result: Avatar realism was strong enough that internal training reviewers focused on the content, not the artifice — the biggest tell was slightly limited hand/body gesture range, not facial or lip-sync quality. Multi-language rendering worked cleanly across all 3 languages tested, with lip-sync holding up even on non-English scripts.
