A Realistic Engagement Workflow

New client kicks off: research their industry and competitors in Perplexity (30 min instead of a half-day), set up a Notion workspace for the engagement, and use Zapier to route the signed contract into your project management and invoicing tools automatically. During check-in calls, Otter.ai captures notes and action items. For any deliverable involving client data, Julius AI turns raw numbers into charts fast, and Claude drafts the narrative report around them — cutting a deliverable that used to take a full day down to a few focused hours of review and polish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI write an entire client deliverable on its own?

It produces a strong first draft from your research and notes, which is genuinely faster than starting blank — but a consultant's judgment, client-specific context, and quality review are still what makes a deliverable client-ready, not the AI draft alone.

How do agencies handle client data privacy with AI tools?

Review each tool's data handling policy and your client contracts before uploading sensitive client data — some agencies restrict which tools can touch client data entirely and use them only for general, non-client-specific research and drafting.

What's the highest-leverage AI tool for a solo consultant?

Claude for deliverable drafting and Perplexity for research typically save the most time per engagement, since research and first-draft writing are usually the largest time sinks for solo consultants without support staff.
