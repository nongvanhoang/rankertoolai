Where Semrush Won

Backlink data depth. With 43 trillion indexed backlinks versus SE Ranking's 3 trillion, Semrush's Backlink Analytics surfaced meaningfully more referring domains and anchor text data on competitive sites — a real advantage for serious link-building outreach or competitive audits in high-authority niches.

Keyword database and content tools. Semrush's larger keyword database (roughly 25.5 billion vs SE Ranking's 5 billion) matters most on niche, long-tail, or non-English markets. Its content marketing suite is also considerably more developed than SE Ranking's basic content module.

The real dividing line: For rank tracking and site auditing — daily SEO work — the two tools are close enough that price should decide. For deep backlink research, large keyword databases, or content marketing tooling, Semrush's premium is justified.
