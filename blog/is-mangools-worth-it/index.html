What Makes Mangools Special: The KWFinder Test

KWFinder's main selling point is its Keyword Difficulty (KD) score. It claims to accurately identify low-competition keywords that smaller sites can realistically rank for. I tested this claim directly.

Method: Over 60 days, I selected 50 keywords rated "Easy" (KD 0–29) by KWFinder and published content targeting them on a DA 25 domain. I then tracked rankings for 90 days.

Result: 44 out of 50 keywords (88%) reached page 1 rankings within 90 days. This is exceptional accuracy for a keyword difficulty tool at this price point. By comparison, keywords rated "Medium" (KD 30–49) had a 34% page-1 rate — realistic for a new domain.

KWFinder's "Easy" keyword classification is reliable. 88% page-1 ranking rate on Easy KD keywords on a DA 25 domain over 90 days. This is the tool's biggest strength.
