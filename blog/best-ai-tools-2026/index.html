How to Choose the Right AI Tool

Start with one AI assistant. Most people need Claude or ChatGPT first — they handle the widest range of tasks. Add category-specific tools (Surfer SEO, Midjourney, ElevenLabs) only once you know your specific bottleneck.

Free tiers go far. Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, ElevenLabs, Canva, and Runway all have free plans that are genuinely useful — not just demos. Start free, upgrade when you hit limits.

Don't subscribe to everything. The average person needs 2-3 AI tools maximum. A generalist AI assistant (Claude), a specialized tool for your primary workflow (Surfer SEO for content marketers, Cursor for developers, Midjourney for designers), and optionally a productivity tool (Notion AI).
