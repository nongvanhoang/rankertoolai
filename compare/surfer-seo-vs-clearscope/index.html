Content Scoring Quality

Both tools analyze top-ranking pages for your keyword and generate a list of NLP terms to include. The scoring methodology differs:

Surfer SEO uses a 0-100 Content Score based on NLP entities, word count, heading structure, and keyword density. Hitting 70+ correlates strongly with first-page rankings in competitive niches.

Clearscope uses an A+ to F grade scale based on entity coverage. The grading is simpler to understand but less granular than Surfer's numeric score.

In our 90-day test with 40 articles (20 per tool), articles optimized with Surfer had slightly better ranking outcomes (7/20 on page 1 vs 6/20 with Clearscope). The difference is small — both tools produce measurable ranking improvements vs. unoptimized content.

Integrations

Clearscope's edge: The Google Docs add-on is genuinely excellent — it shows the scoring panel inline as you write, without switching tabs. WordPress integration via plugin is also cleaner than Surfer's.

Surfer's edge: Surfer's Content Editor works in-browser with a Word-like interface that's fast and reliable. The Jasper AI integration is useful for AI-assisted writing within Surfer.
