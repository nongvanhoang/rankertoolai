Is Writesonic cheaper than Copy.ai?
Yes. Writesonic's paid entry plan is $16/month (Freelancer, unlimited words) versus Copy.ai's $36/month starter plan — more than 2× cheaper. Writesonic's free plan also gives 10,000 words per month, compared to Copy.ai's 2,000 word limit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better for blog posts, Writesonic or Copy.ai?

Writesonic is slightly better for blog posts — its Article Writer 5.0 is specifically designed for long-form SEO content with good structure and keyword integration. Copy.ai's blog tools are solid but the workflow is better suited for shorter content or systematic bulk creation.

Does Copy.ai have workflow automation?

Yes — Copy.ai's AI Workflows is a standout feature that lets you chain AI tasks together (e.g., research → outline → draft → SEO optimize) automatically. Writesonic doesn't have equivalent workflow automation. For teams that need scalable, systematic content production, Copy.ai's workflows are a significant advantage.

Which is better for social media, Writesonic or Copy.ai?

Both excel at social media content. Copy.ai's social media templates and multi-platform angle testing (generate 10 variations of a LinkedIn post) give it a slight edge for social content volume. Writesonic's Chatsonic helps with researching trending topics before writing social content.

Which AI writing tool is best for SEO?

For SEO content specifically, Writesonic has slightly better tools — particularly when used with Surfer SEO integration. Copy.ai focuses more on conversion copywriting and less on search optimization. Neither matches Jasper's dedicated SEO writing features, but both are solid for creating optimized blog content.
