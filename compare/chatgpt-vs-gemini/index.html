ChatGPT wins on writing quality, coding, and raw reasoning performance. Gemini wins when you're deep in the Google ecosystem — Gmail, Docs, Meet, and Drive integration is unmatched. Writing Quality: ChatGPT Leads

On our standard writing benchmark (blog intro, business email, persuasive argument, product description), ChatGPT scored an average of 8.9/10 vs Gemini's 8.2/10. ChatGPT's prose is more varied, better paced, and produces more natural-sounding long-form content. Gemini produces clean, accurate writing but can feel formulaic for creative tasks.

For factual business writing and email drafting, Gemini holds its own. For blog content, storytelling, and marketing copy, ChatGPT is noticeably better.

Coding: ChatGPT Still Ahead

On our 20-task coding benchmark (Python debugging, TypeScript generation, React components, SQL queries), ChatGPT (GPT-4o) solved 17/20 correctly on first attempt vs Gemini's 14/20. ChatGPT also explains its code more clearly and is better at debugging complex logic errors. Gemini is good but not yet at GPT-4o's level for coding tasks.

Google Workspace Integration: Gemini Dominates

This is Gemini's killer feature. Within Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Meet, and Drive, Gemini is deeply embedded — draft emails from brief prompts, summarize long documents, generate formulas, and summarize meeting transcripts automatically. ChatGPT can access Google services via plugins, but the experience is fragmented compared to Gemini's native integration. If you live in Google Workspace, Gemini is transformative.

Context Window: Gemini's Secret Weapon

Gemini 1.5 Pro offers a 1 million token context window — the largest available in any mainstream AI. This means you can upload an entire codebase, a 300-page document, or hours of video for analysis. ChatGPT's 128K token window is generous but pales in comparison for truly long-context tasks. For research, legal review, or large codebase analysis, Gemini's context advantage is decisive.

Choose ChatGPT if you:

  • Need best-in-class writing quality
  • Do significant coding or debugging work
  • Want DALL-E 3 image generation built-in
  • Use third-party plugins or integrations
  • Are not deeply invested in Google Workspace
  • Need the best general-purpose AI assistant

Choose Gemini if you:

  • Use Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Meet daily
  • Need to process very long documents (1M token context)
  • Want native Google Search integration
  • Are on a Google One subscription already
  • Need multimodal video/audio analysis
  • Are a Google Workspace admin for your team

FAQ

Is ChatGPT better than Gemini overall?

For most general tasks — yes. ChatGPT scores higher on writing quality, coding, and reasoning. Gemini is better specifically for Google Workspace users and very long-context document tasks.

Which AI is free and better?

Both have free plans. ChatGPT Free gives limited GPT-4o access. Gemini Free gives Gemini 1.5 Flash. For free-tier quality, they're roughly equivalent — though Gemini Free's Google integration works on the free plan too.

Should I use both?

Many power users do. ChatGPT for writing and coding tasks, Gemini for Google Workspace tasks and long-document analysis. Both have free tiers so using both costs nothing.

Our Pick: ChatGPT Plus

The better all-around assistant for most users. GPT-4o + DALL-E 3 + Advanced Data Analysis at $20/mo.
